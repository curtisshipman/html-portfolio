The Best Movies According to Curtis

My top movies of all-time, by category.

The Princess Bride

This is my favorite feel good movie. It's like comfort food for my soul.

Jacob's Ladder (Directors Cut if you can get it)

This is my favorite thriller. It's intense, and a very dark, but not quite a horror movie.

The Transporter

This is my favorite action movie. This is a great driving and fight choreography movie, and one of Jason Statham's early films that launched his career.

Hard Boiled

This is my favorite shooter movie. This is the John Woo film starring Chow Yun-fat that changed how shooter movies in Hollywood are made. Directors are still following his action style today.
